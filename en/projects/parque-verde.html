Quirihue Green Urban Park

AutoCAD | PVsyst | DIALux | BESS

Comprehensive electrical engineering design for a major Urban Park in the Quirihue commune. This project focuses on sustainability and energy autonomy, integrating renewable generation and advanced storage.

Technical Details

  • Photovoltaic System: Design of a 281 kWp array to cover the park's energy demand.
  • Storage (BESS): Sizing of a battery system for backup and demand management.
  • Efficient Lighting: Lighting and electrical planning to guarantee safety and efficiency.

Special Achievement

I received Special Honors from my tutor in the "Impact Internships" program due to technical excellence and management capability demonstrated during this project.
